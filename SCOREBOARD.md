## Verdicts

- **#1 — ACTIVE.** Fastest path to cash: warmest network (Dad), easiest manual delivery, weekly-felt
  pain, provable ROI (one recovered job pays for months of service). Weakness: crowded "AI receptionist"
  space and mediocre SaaS ceiling — this is our **cash-flow and market-entry play**, not necessarily the
  end-state company. Deep dive: [`opportunities/01-lead-rescue-contractors.md`](opportunities/01-lead-rescue-contractors.md)
- **#2/#3 — DISCOVERY ONLY.** Better SaaS ceiling, real data moat (document corpus + vendor/SKU mappings),
  Mom's unfair advantage. But longer sales cycle and higher build cost — wrong first play with no capital.
  Run interviews now, sell later, fund it with #1 revenue. Deep dive: [`opportunities/02-po-order-entry.md`](opportunities/02-po-order-entry.md)
- **#4 — BACKLOG.** Real pain but incumbents exist (myCOI, SmartCompliance) and urgency is
  audit-driven (spiky). Natural **upsell** to GCs we meet through #1, not a first offer.
- **#5 — BACKLOG.** Strong pain, but buyers are the same contractors as #1 — bundle later as an
  upsell ("we also turn your field notes into change orders") instead of a separate sale.
