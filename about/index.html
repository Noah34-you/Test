Why FitCo Exists

Most men don't struggle because their bodies are hard to fit. They struggle because most pants were never designed around real body proportions.

FitCo exists to fix that.

Why pants fit so badly

Most men buy pants by waist size. That's the problem. Waist size doesn't tell you how pants fit everywhere else.

  • thigh size
  • taper preference
  • inseam
  • body proportions

Two men can wear the same waist size and need completely different fits.

Tight in the thighs

Your waist fits but your thighs don't. Common for men with athletic builds.

Waist gaps

Your hips fit but the back gapes open. Most brands don't account for this.

Wrong length

Inseam varies by brand, cut, and even fabric. Most men settle for hemming pants that should have fit from the start.

Sizing was never built for everyone

Most brands still use sizing from decades ago. That creates constant frustration. One brand fits well. Another fits terribly. Same waist size, completely different fit.

The reality is simple: a 32 waist does not mean the same thing across brands, cuts, or countries. Men are left guessing, ordering multiple sizes, and returning what does not work.
